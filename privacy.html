Privacy Policy

Fal Dünyası is an entertainment app for AI-assisted Turkish coffee cup readings. This policy explains what data the app may process and why.

Entertainment Only

Coffee readings are for entertainment and reflection. The app does not provide medical, legal, financial, safety, or life-critical advice.

Data We Process

  • Photos or videos: You may capture or choose a coffee cup photo so the app can generate a reading. In production, cup photos may be uploaded to private storage for AI analysis.
  • User ID: The app may use an anonymous Supabase user identifier and a RevenueCat app user identifier to manage readings, quota, and premium access.
  • Purchase history: Premium status, restore, and entitlement state may be processed through Apple in-app purchases and RevenueCat.
  • Local app state: The app may keep small local data such as reading quota state, cached reading results, favorite status, and compressed cup thumbnails for history.

Analytics and Diagnostics

Future analytics or crash reporting may be used to improve reliability and product quality. Photo content and free-form personal text should not be sent to analytics payloads. If analytics or crash reporting is enabled, the App Store privacy form and this policy should be reviewed before release.

Payments

Purchases are handled by Apple. Subscription access and restore state may be managed through RevenueCat. The app does not store your full payment card details.

Deleting Data

You can delete your account and associated reading data directly in the app: open Settings and choose to delete your account data. You may also email the support contact on the support page to request deletion; include your app user identifier so we can locate eligible records.

Contact

For privacy or support requests, use the support contact listed on the support page.
